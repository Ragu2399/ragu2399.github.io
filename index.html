About Me

I am a robotics engineer currently working as a Project Associate in the Image-Guided Robotic Systems (IGRS) Group at HTICâ€“IIT Madras, focusing on robot motion planning and reinforcement learning. My work involves developing motion planners for robotic-assisted surgeries, including percutaneous interventional spinal procedures, and creating reinforcement learning-based visual servoing modules used in autonomous antenatal ultrasound scanning. I have well-rounded experience in building modules for robotic spine surgery, from developing computational frameworks to deploying and validating robots in cadaveric settings.
