Hi, I'm Ragu

About Me

I'm a passionate robotics engineer driven by the challenge of bringing robots to life and solving real-world problems with intelligent, practical solutions. Currently, I work as a robotics engineer with the Image-Guided Robotic Systems Group at HTIC–IIT Madras , where I develop intelligent systems at the intersection of artificial intelligence, healthcare, and medical robotics. My work involves developing motion planners for robotic systems and building AI agents from the ground up including architecture design, training, and deployment—for a wide range of real-world applications.

Projects

Hybrid-Spine Surgical Motion Planner

Engineered a hybrid motion planner combining geometric and constrained sampling methods for spine surgical procedures.

The motion planner has been tested and validated in a cadaver setting.
